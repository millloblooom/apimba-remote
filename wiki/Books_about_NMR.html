Books about NMR

From apimba
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Here are some of the best books about NMR. Because of the wealth of information about NMR online, there is no need for basic or even somewhat advanced NMR books in your library. Books to keep on your shelf are detailed experimental protocols, compendia of spectra, summaries of the latest research and explorations of niche topics.

Must Have

150 and More Basic NMR Experiments: A Practical Course 2nd Completely Revised and Enlarged Edition by Siegmar Braun, Hans-Otto Kalinowski, Stefan Berger (1998) ISBN-13: 978-3527295128 ISBN-10: 3527295127

200 and More NMR Experiments: A Practical Course 1st Edition by Stefan Berger, Siegmar Braun (2004) ISBN-13: 978-3527310678 ISBN-10: 3527310673
