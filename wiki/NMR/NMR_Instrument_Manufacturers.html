list of current 2020 high field manufacturers

  • Bruker - highest field, latest technology
  • Jeol - medium field
  • QoneTec - cheap 400 and 600MHz instruments, switzerland

list of current 2020 table-top manufacturers (permanent magnets)

  • Bruker Fourier
  • Thermofisher - 42 ($4k) and 82MHz ($52k) with small volume detection
  • Anasazi - 60 and 90MHz
  • Aspect Imaging - includes MRI instruments
  • Magritek - 43, 60, 80 MHz
  • Nanalysis - 60 ($47k)and 100MHz ($125k)
  • Oxford Instruments - 2,12 and 20MHz solid state, 60MHz liquid (Pulsar)
  • Resonance Systems
  • SpinCore - portable NMR
  • Spinlock - oil and food analysis, probably solid-state NMR
