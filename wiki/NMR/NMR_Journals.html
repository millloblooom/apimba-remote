NMR Journals

From apimba
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Articles in scientific journals that require payment to view cannot be trusted since they have limited exposure and fewer opportunities to have their results and conclusions confirmed. The fact that they only allow a viewer to look at the abstract reinforces their dodgy nature. This page will include them in the lists for information purposes, but their use for research is of questionable value.

Open access journals

  • Concepts in Magnetic Resonance Part A -stopped publishing in 2018, moved to different publisher 2020 with fewer issues, free archive of old issues
  • Concepts in Magnetic Resonance Part B- stopped publishing in 2018, moved to different publisher 2020 with fewer issues, free archive of old issues
  • Magnetic Resonance in Solids
  • Spectroscopy Europe Asia - have to register to read but it is open access

Closed access journals

  • Annual Reports on NMR Spectroscopy - all articles are pay-to-read
  • Applied Magnetic Resonance - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • Biomolecular NMR Assignments - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • eMagRes - all articles are pay-to-read
  • IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging -some articles are open access, most are pay-to-read
  • Journal of Magnetic Resonance - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • Journal of Magnetic Resonance Imaging (JMRI) - issues from 2000 to 2015 are open access, before and after that are pay-to-read
  • Journal of Biomolecular NMR - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • Magnetic Resonance in Chemistry - all articles are pay-to-read
  • Magnetic Resonance in Medicine - issues from 1999 to 2014 are open access, before and after that are pay-to-read
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • Magnetic Resonance Materials in Physics, Biology and Medicine - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • NMR in Biomedicine - some articles are open access, some are pay-to-read
  • Progress in Nuclear Magnetic Resonance Spectroscopy - says it supports open access but only a few articles are open, most are pay-to-read
  • Solid State Nuclear Magnetic Resonance - says it supports open access but almost all articles are pay-to-read



No longer published journals

  • Current Protocols in Magnetic Resonance Imaging - last issue in 2008, final issue is open access, older issues are pay-to-read
